  - **PVP was almost entirely fabricated placeholder content**: "Free-For-All" isn't a real term (the actual mode is "King of the Hill," attack-the-Leader-for-a-bonus-card, not a generic FFA — see the 2026-07-17 rulepop follow-up below for the corrected scaling-Health/team-mode details); "Reactive cards" isn't a real card category (the only types are Instant Action/Roll Phase Action/Main Phase Action/Hero Upgrade); hero draft/snake-draft/fist-reveal procedures don't exist (the book only bans duplicate heroes and leaves selection method to the group); Income Phase is +1 CP not +2, CP cap is 15 not 10; PVP setup draws 4 cards not 5; "re-roll ties" on the Start Player roll is invented.

Rewrote all eight to match `STATUS_DEFS` and the rulebook. Also found `status-stun` and `status-first-strike` described status effects that don't exist in this game at all — repurposed `status-stun` into a real one (Knockdown) and deleted `status-first-strike` outright. Deleted `missions-loot`, which described a system that doesn't exist (duplicate/confusion of the already-correct `boost-tiles` entry). Fixed `missions-setup`'s starting-HP claim (it's a shared table by hero count — 45/30/25/20 — not read off each hero's own board) and its Crisis Clock setup (randomized by a die roll, not "set to 0"). Fixed `phase-move` (unlimited movement distance, not "1-2 spaces"; no "attacks of opportunity" concept) and `phase-enemy-upkeep` (Bleed still needs its 1d6 roll on enemies too, doesn't auto-clear).
  - **Adventures**: `adv-minions` and `adv-tiles` were largely invented — Minions don't roam the map or spawn 1-per-hero (1 Minion is drawn per explored tile, engaging that hero directly), and the dungeon isn't a shuffled/rotated tile-laying deck (it's a fixed layout from the Scenario Card). Fixed `adv-gold` (spent at Rosella's Shop on Loot cards, not a fabricated "Camp" for healing), `adv-loot`/`boss-loot` consistency (Boss Loot is awarded on a loss too, not just a win; only discarded cards score points), `salves` (revival restores Health only, no "+2 CP" that isn't in either rulebook), and `portal-crawl`'s win condition (must defeat the Level IV Minion at the Boss Portal with no heroes still Engaged, not just "reach" it).
